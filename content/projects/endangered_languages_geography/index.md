date: 2022

https://github.com/DavidRambo/endangered_languages_geography

Analysis and visualization of the geographical distribution of endangered and extinct languages using a dataset on kaggle.com.
Everything is contained in a Jupyter notebook, which uses Pandas and Numpy for the analysis and Matplotlib for an interactive planetary map view of the languages.
